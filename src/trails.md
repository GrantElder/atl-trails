### Stone Mountain Trail — Atlanta to Stone Mountain Park

**~17–19.5 miles** · Centennial Olympic Park → Stone Mountain Park

A multiuse path connecting downtown Atlanta to Stone Mountain Park. Suitable for walking, running, biking. Open year-round, family-friendly, leashed dogs welcome.

- **Start** — Centennial Olympic Park (downtown)
- **Route** — Freedom Park → Candler Park, Lake Claire (on-street) → Decatur (side path) → Clarkston (rails-to-trails, crosses I-285) → Stone Mountain Village (side path) → Stone Mountain Park (south entrance)
- **Time** — ~6–7 hours on foot
- **Access** — Carter Center, King Center, MARTA Avondale & Eastlake

Sources: [PATH Foundation](https://www.pathfoundation.org/stone-mountain-trail), [AllTrails](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/georgia/stone-mountain-trail-atlanta-to-stone-mountain-park)
